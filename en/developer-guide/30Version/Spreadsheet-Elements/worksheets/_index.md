A worksheet is a collection of cells where you store and manipulate data. Each Excel workbook can contain multiple worksheets.

**Worksheet Operations**

- [Get all worksheets](/cells/worksheets/get-all/) – Retrieves the complete list of worksheets in a workbook.  
- [Get a worksheet in a specific format](/cells/worksheets/get/) – Returns a worksheet in JSON, XML, CSV, or other supported formats.  
- [Export a worksheet area in different formats](/cells/worksheets/area-to-different-formats/) – Converts a selected cell range to PDF, HTML, or image formats.  
- [Export a worksheet page in different formats](/cells/get-worksheet-for-page-index/) – Retrieves a particular page of a worksheet as PDF, PNG, or other formats.  
- [Convert a worksheet to an image](/cells/worksheets/to-image/) – Renders the entire worksheet as a PNG, JPEG, or BMP image.  
- [Add a new worksheet](/cells/worksheets/add/) – Creates an empty worksheet within the specified workbook.  
- [Add a background to a worksheet](/cells/worksheets/background/add/) – Applies a background image to the chosen worksheet.  
- [Sort data in a worksheet](/cells/worksheets/sort-data/) – Sorts rows or columns based on defined criteria.  
- [Hide a worksheet](/cells/worksheets/hide/) – Makes the selected worksheet invisible without deleting it.  
- [Unhide a worksheet](/cells/worksheets/unhide/) – Restores visibility to a previously hidden worksheet.  
- [Move a worksheet](/cells/worksheets/move/) – Changes the position of a worksheet within the workbook.  
- [Calculate a worksheet formula](/cells/worksheets/calculate-formula/) – Evaluates formulas in the worksheet and returns the results.  
- [Copy a worksheet](/cells/worksheets/copy/) – Duplicates an existing worksheet into the same or another workbook.  
- [Rename a worksheet](/cells/worksheets/rename/) – Changes the name of an existing worksheet.  
- [Update worksheet properties](/cells/worksheets/update-properties/) – Modifies properties such as tab color, visibility, and protection settings.  
- [Set worksheet zoom](/cells/worksheets/zoom/) – Adjusts the zoom level for the worksheet view.  
- [Freeze panes in a worksheet](/cells/worksheets/freeze-panes/) – Locks rows and/or columns so they remain visible while scrolling.  
- [Unfreeze panes in a worksheet](/cells/worksheets/unfreeze-panes/) – Removes any existing pane‑freezing settings.  
- [Delete a worksheet](/cells/worksheets/delete/) – Removes the specified worksheet from the workbook.  
- [Delete a worksheet background](/cells/worksheets/background/delete/) – Removes the background image from a worksheet.  
- [Autofit a worksheet](/cells/worksheets/autofit/) – Automatically adjusts column widths and row heights to fit the content.
